The Secret Formula!

Description

The Krabby Patty is made out of a frozen meatless burger with buns, the patty, pickles, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, ketchup, mustard, and onions and with other elements according to a Krabby Patty secret formula.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound crab meat
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Hot sauce/Tabasco sauce to taste
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups bread crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons red pepper
  • 1 quart oil for frying
  • Hamburger buns (whatever you like) lightly toasted
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • American Cheese
  • Dill pickle slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

    Directions for Patties

  1. Mix crab, egg, breadcrumbs, mayonnaise, chives, mustard, lemon juice, celery seed, onion powder, pepper and hot sauce in a large bowl. Form into 6 patties.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until the butter stops foaming. Cook the patties until golden brown, about 4 minutes per side.

    Directions for onion rings

  1. Place the onions, eggs, and flour into separate shallow bowls.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy skillet or deep-fryer to 365 degrees F (180 degrees C). Dip rings of onion into flour, then into the egg, and then into the bread crumb mixture, shaking off any excess after each dip.
  3. Carefully drop each onion ring into the hot oil, and fry for about 30 seconds on each side, or until golden. Remove from hot oil to paper towels to drain.
